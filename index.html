Scooty rental

Call ahead the morning of July 11 to hold a scooter for pickup right after you arrive.

  • LocoRides — Varkala's first govt-authorised rental, clean paperwork. Map ↗
  • Unnikannan Travel Company — Temple Road, 4.7★, run personally by Rohith. Map ↗

Carry your original driving license + Aadhaar/passport. Kerala enforces the double-helmet rule strictly. Return by ~2:00 PM on Day 4.

Monsoon notes

July is peak monsoon — mornings tend to be clearer, showers more likely by late afternoon.

  • Carry a light poncho on the scooty rather than an umbrella.
  • Cliff-edge paths get slippery after rain — walk, don't ride, on the pedestrian stretch.
  • Kappil Lake kayaking depends on conditions on the day — decide with the operator rather than pre-booking.
  • Jatayu Earth's Center entry is cash only — no cards or UPI at the counter.
